Meaningful Climate Action

  • Mobilizing Canadians to not only meet, but exceed our Paris Agreement climate targets.
  • Expanding carbon pricing to include all industries, and to better account for all forms of GHGs
  • Creating a national, permanent public transit fund to support projects across Canada
  • Transitioning Canada's dependence on fossil fuels with strategic investments, retraining and by phasing out subsidies
  • Providing grants and incentives to help make Canada a world leader in sustainable technology and innovation
  • Stopping the expansion of the TransMountain pipeline

Public Health

  • Investing in Canada's public healthcare, with a focus on preventative care and vastly improving mental health coverage
  • Combatting the opioid crisis with support for public addictions treatment facilities
  • Supporting a transition to universal Pharmacare and dental care, beginning with those without private insurance coverage
  • Expanding public health awareness campaigns on topics such as substance use (including tobacco and vaping)
