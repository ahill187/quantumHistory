About Austen

Austen is a public engagement and communication consultant who works primarily with public sector organizations, including the BC Government and municipalities across western Canada.

His focus is on projects in the transportation and public health sectors, with a passion for sustainability threaded throughout much of his work. He has extensive experience working with Indigenous communities, and was co-recipient of the 2019 International Association for Public Participation (IAP2) Core Values Award for Indigenous Engagement.
