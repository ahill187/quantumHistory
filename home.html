Meaningful Climate Action

Canada's current government ran on a platform of climate action: and credit should be given where credit is due. The Trudeau government has instituted federal carbon pricing, and has rolled back or prevented many of the damaging environmental policies of its preceding government.

And yet, its carbon pricing policy goes easy on many of Canada's worst polluters. Our government remains comitted to the unfettered expansion of Canada's oil and gas industry - to the point of spending billions of dollars to purchase a struggling pipeline and its proposed expansion. We are nowhere near on track to meeting our Paris Agreement climate commitments, which many climate scientists argue are already far too low to avoid catastrophic climate change.
